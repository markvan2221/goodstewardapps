Request Data Deletion

How to delete your data from ScripturePicture, a game by Good Steward Apps.

What data we hold

ScripturePicture runs almost entirely on your device. Your game progress — player names, scores, badges, best times and settings — is stored only on your phone and is never sent to us. If you simply uninstall the app, all of that on-device data is removed with it.

The only data we ever hold on our servers is a voluntary World Leaderboard entry — and only if you chose to tap "Submit my best" in the app. That entry contains:

  • the player name (nickname) you submitted;
  • your game statistics (score, scriptures found, Cackle Berries, badges) and chosen language; and
  • a random player identifier generated by the app (it does not identify your device or you).

How to request deletion

To have your World Leaderboard entry permanently deleted, email us at feedback@goodstewardapps.com with:

  1. the subject line "ScripturePicture — delete my leaderboard entry", and
  2. the player name you used on the leaderboard (so we can find your entry).

We will remove your entry from our database, and it will no longer appear on the public leaderboard. We aim to action deletion requests within 30 days.

What is deleted and what is kept

Deleted: your entire World Leaderboard entry — the player name, statistics, language and random identifier listed above. Nothing about that entry is retained after deletion.

Kept: nothing else. We hold no other personal data about you. Any game progress stays on your own device and is under your control — reset it in the app's "New Game" option, or uninstall the app to remove it entirely.
