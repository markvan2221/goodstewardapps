Frequently asked

How do I check a spot on the map?
Pan and zoom to the place, then press and hold until the ring fills.

How do I change the language?
Open Settings from the home screen. Ten languages are available, with 11 public-domain Bible translations — including Japanese, Chinese and Arabic.

Is my data collected?
No — the app is designed to run entirely on your device. See our privacy policy.
