Privacy Policy for ScripturePicture

Last updated: 11 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Good Steward ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the mobile game ScripturePicture (the "App"). We are based in Australia.

We have designed ScripturePicture to respect your privacy. In short: the App works entirely on your device and does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

1. The short version

  • We do not collect personal information through normal gameplay.
  • We do not have any servers or online accounts — there is no backend.
  • We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking technologies.
  • We do not share, sell, or transfer any data to third parties.
  • Everything the App needs ships inside the App itself.
  • The only information we ever receive is what you choose to send us through our optional "Leave Feedback" form (see Section 4).

2. Information we do not collect

During normal gameplay, ScripturePicture does not ask for, collect, or transmit any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or any contact details.
  • Your location.
  • Device identifiers, advertising IDs, or cookies.
  • Usage analytics, crash reports, or diagnostic data.
  • Any other personal or sensitive information.

The App does not require you to create an account or sign in. The only time we receive any information is if you voluntarily submit it through our "Leave Feedback" form — see Section 4.

3. Information stored only on your device

To let you play, the App saves certain game data locally on your device using your device's standard on-device storage. This includes things like:

  • Your player profile name(s) and game progress.
  • Scriptures and hidden items you have found.
  • Your score, badges, and best times.
  • Your settings, such as the chosen Bible language and music volume.

This information never leaves your device. We cannot see it, and it is not sent to us or to anyone else. If you delete the App, this locally stored data is removed along with it. Any player names you choose are entirely up to you — you do not need to use a real name.

4. Feedback you choose to send us

The App includes an optional "Leave Feedback" link. Tapping it opens a feedback page in your browser on our website, where you can enter a name, an optional email address, a star rating and a comment. Using it is entirely voluntary — you never have to.

When you press send, your feedback is submitted to our website and emailed to us. We use a third-party email delivery provider (Resend), acting on our behalf solely to deliver that message to our inbox; we do not store your feedback in any database. As with any visit to a website, our hosting and security provider (Cloudflare) also processes standard technical request information, such as your IP address, in order to serve the page and protect against abuse.

The information we receive is the name, rating and comment you type, plus — only if you choose to provide it — your email address, which we use to reply to you. If you provide your email address or any other contact details:

  • They will never be sold, rented, or shared with any external party.
  • They will be used only to read and reply to your feedback, or to notify you about updates and new software from Good Steward.
  • You can ask us to delete your feedback at any time by contacting us using the details in Section 11.

5. Children's privacy

ScripturePicture is intended for a general audience of all ages and is suitable for everyone. It is not specifically directed at children, and because the App does not collect any personal information from anyone, it does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has somehow provided us with personal information, please contact us — although, by design, the App provides us with no way to receive such information.

6. Third-party services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, social media, or data-collection services.

The App is distributed through the Apple App Store and/or Google Play Store. Those platforms have their own privacy policies governing downloads, purchases, and platform-level data, which are outside our control. We encourage you to review:

  • Apple's Privacy Policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
  • Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

7. Data security

Other than any feedback you voluntarily send us (Section 4), we do not collect or store your information on any server, so there is virtually no personal data held by us that could be exposed in a data breach. Game data stays on your device and is protected by your device's own security measures.

8. Your rights

Other than the feedback you may have voluntarily sent us (Section 4), we do not hold any of your personal information, so there is generally nothing for us to access, correct, or delete on your behalf. You remain in full control of the game data stored on your device: you can clear it at any time by resetting your progress within the App or by uninstalling the App. If you have sent us feedback, you may ask us to delete it at any time using the contact details in Section 11.

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under privacy laws such as the Australian Privacy Principles (under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth)), the EU/UK General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), or the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Because we collect virtually no personal information, most of these rights are not engaged — but you are welcome to contact us with any questions.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example if we add new features. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this Policy and, where appropriate, provide notice within the App. Continued use of the App after an update means you accept the revised Policy.

10. Governing law

This Privacy Policy is governed by the laws of Australia.
