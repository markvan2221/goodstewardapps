How it works

Read, search, discover

ScripturePicture invites players to connect Bible passages with visual scenes. It is designed to be calm, curious and rewarding rather than rushed.

Privacy-minded

No tracking by design

The app is being designed to work on-device without accounts, advertising or analytics. See the privacy policy for details.
