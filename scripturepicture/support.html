Frequently asked

How do I check a spot on the map?
Pan and zoom to the place, then press and hold until the ring fills.

How do I change the language?
Open Settings from the home screen — eight languages are available, including 日本語.

Is my data collected?
No — the app runs entirely on your device. See our privacy policy.
